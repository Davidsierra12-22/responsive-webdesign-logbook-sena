# Logbook Entry #1 - Problem Exploration

## 1. What problem did I choose and why?

I chose "Low productivity in remote work settings." I selected this problem because remote work is the standard norm in the software development industry (ADSO). As future developers, we need to understand how to manage our time efficiently from home and overcome common remote distractions to deliver high-quality code.

---

## 2. What contexts or data influenced my choice?

My choice was influenced by the current software industry trends post-pandemic. Many tech companies report a decline in sprint completion rates when teams lack organization. Additionally, personal academic experiences studying development from home showed me how easy it is to lose focus without a structured routine.

---

## 3. What are the possible solutions I identified?

I identified three main areas of solution: time-management frameworks (like the Pomodoro Technique), creating professional boundaries (both digital and physical environments), and leveraging Agile tracking tools to maintain accountability without micro-management.

---

## 4. What questions or doubts do I have?

My main question is how to best structure the HTML semantic sections in the next stage so that the solutions look visually clean, engaging, and fully responsive for users reading the guide on mobile devices.
